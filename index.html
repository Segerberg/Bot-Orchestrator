Welcome to MkDocs

Bot-Orchestrator is a suite that offers the following resources to research programs wanting to study networks in laboratory experiments. The application allows researchers to add bots to a video conference to increase the perceived number of participants in a test session. Additionally, individual bots can send text messages via a chat function during the video conference. We hope to include voice messaging in the future. Moreover, research programs can manipulate the current messaging feature to induce psychological variables or create different social situations of interest.
